Technical Architecture

A modern, scalable ML pipeline ready for integration

  • Open Earth Observation data (Sentinel-2, Landsat)
  • NDVI, EVI, and spectral indices time series
  • Climate reanalysis (ERA5, CHIRPS)
  • DEM derivatives (elevation, slope, aspect)
  • SoilGrids for texture and pH covariates
  • Random Forest / XGBoost with hyperparameter tuning
  • Spatial cross-validation to avoid overfitting

Why Random Forest & XGBoost?

According to the STEROPES final synthesis report, these models outperform others for SOC prediction when combined with temporal satellite data. They handle non-linear relationships and are robust to overfitting.
